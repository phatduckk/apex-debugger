when the input/ouput type is 24v here's what the 2 character values mean

These strings are basically:
[Control Mode][State][Extra Flag]


Value:Meaning
AOF: Auto mode, currently OFF
AON: Auto mode, currently ON
ATO: Auto mode, output is ON (older/alt encoding)
AOF: Auto OFF (most common “off” state)
FON: Forced ON (manual override ON)
FOF: Forced OFF (manual override OFF)
OFF: Explicit OFF (fallback / no program)
ON: Explicit ON
